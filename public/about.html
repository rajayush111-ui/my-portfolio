About Me

I plan my time well, balance studies with physical activity, and believe in discipline, self-improvement, and long-term consistency.

I have a strong interest in technology, especially cybersecurity and ethical hacking, and I prefer learning through structured roadmaps and practical projects. I like understanding concepts deeply, asking “why” instead of memorizing answers, and I care about building a strong profile through projects, certifications, and continuous learning.

I also value purpose along with stability. I want to serve in meaningful roles, possibly in government or defense, while keeping strong private-sector options as a backup. I am realistic about my choices, open to improving myself, and determined to grow into a skilled, responsible, and dependable professional.
